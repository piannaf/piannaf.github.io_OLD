SineMaster Lite

Jan 30, 2010

SineMaster Lite is the result of learning a lot of the features of C# as well as a substantial amount of .NET pertaining to WPF.

After spending a lot of time dissecting Microsoft sample code, I wanted to try my hand at a simple program. This is a milestone to a larger project I planned: a game called SineMaster. In SineMaster, you would be given the image of a waveform and need to reproduce it by combining sine waves.
