Naive Spell Checker

Jul 12, 2010

This naive spell checker was a three part project over the course of the semester. This was very easy because I had taught myself C# the summer before.

Simply give it a dictionary and a document and it will tell you where you went wrong in the document. It will also offer suggested words based on insertion, deletion, substitution, and transposition errors (but not a combination of errors).
