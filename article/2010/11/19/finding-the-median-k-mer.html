Finding the Median k-mer

Nov 19, 2010

For this algorithms assignment, we had to incrementally optimize an algorithm for finding the median k-mer of a DNA sequence.

Pseudo-code was given for a naive implementation so I first implemented that. Then, I added the branch-and-bound mechanism which significantly sped things up. Then, I implemented the trie to store k-mers which allowed me to optimize the algorithm to search promising k-mers first which sped things up slightly more.
