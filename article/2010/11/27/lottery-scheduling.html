Lottery Scheduling

Nov 27, 2010

To learn about thread scheduling we had to modify the Pth scheduler to implement fairness across all threads in a single process using a lottery mechanism.

The policy to ensure fairness is “fair share with priorities” as described in the design specification.

If I were to do this again, I would not use malloc and friends — they are way to slow for hundreds of tickets.
