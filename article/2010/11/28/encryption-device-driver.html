Encryption Device Driver

Nov 28, 2010

We had to implement a linux device driver interface to an encryption ‘coprocessor’. The driver performs a XOR-based stream cipher since we didn’t have special encryption hardware.

By far, the most interesting and most fun assignment I’ve ever had. The lectures consisted of just about zero information about how to go about this so there was a fair bit of research to do.

I decided to make full use of the Linux kernel linked list and circular buffer macros.
