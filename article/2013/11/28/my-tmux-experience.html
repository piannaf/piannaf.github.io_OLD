My tmux Experience

Nov 28, 2013

I have become a lot more productive since I started using tmux.

Before I knew about tmux, I had been using PuttyCM but, as my frustration with it's suboptimal use of screen real estate and frequent crashing grew, I looked for other solutions.

It was by accident that I noticed a friend and colleague's screen one day when it looked liked the entire screen was filled with multiple terminals and only a thin line separating each one. It was beautiful, and it was tmux.

When I changed jobs, I heard a few colleagues talk about screen but they used it for very limited purpose. I created this presentation to show them what tmux was and what I was doing with it.
